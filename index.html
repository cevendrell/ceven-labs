LayOvr

Arrive sharp, anywhere.

A science-backed jet lag companion for business travelers. LayOvr builds a personalized circadian plan around your flights and meetings — telling you when to sleep, nap, eat, get light, and take caffeine so you peak exactly when it matters.

  • Meeting-anchored optimization — plan computed backward from your key moment
  • Multi-leg trip support with layover intelligence and lounge guidance
  • Real-time flight lookup and delay re-planning
  • HealthKit personalization — adapts to your actual sleep patterns
  • Offline circadian engine — works in airplane mode

About Ceven Labs

Ceven Labs is an independent iOS studio based in Aarhus, Denmark. We exist to build focused, premium tools that solve real problems for people who are always on the move.

We believe that great software is specific. Every feature we ship solves a clear problem — nothing more. Our apps are grounded in science, designed with care, and built to work offline when it matters most.
