We're an independent iOS studio from Aarhus, Denmark. We build focused, premium tools — one problem at a time, with obsessive care for detail.

Our mission

Why Ceven Labs exists

Most software is built to be broad. We built Ceven Labs to go deep — to find one specific, real problem, and solve it better than anyone else.

Our first target: jet lag. Not generic sleep tips, but a proper, personalized circadian plan that accounts for your exact flights, your key meetings, and how your body actually works across timezones.

We believe the best tools are built by people who are personally frustrated by the problem they're solving. We travel. We've shown up jet-lagged to meetings that mattered. LayOvr is what we needed.

🎯

Specific over generic

We don't build apps that try to serve everyone. We pick one problem, one user, and go deep until the solution is genuinely better than anything else available.

🔒

Privacy by design

Your health data and travel plans stay on your device. We built the app without a backend on purpose — it's not a cost decision, it's a principle.

⚡

Offline-first

The core features work in airplane mode. That's not a nice-to-have when you're 35,000 feet above the Atlantic — it's the whole point.
