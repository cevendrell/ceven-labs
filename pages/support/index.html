Getting started

LayOvr is a science-backed jet lag companion for business travelers. It builds a personalized daily plan around your flights and meetings — telling you when to sleep, nap, get light, avoid light, and cut off caffeine — so you arrive at your destination ready to perform when it matters. It's designed for frequent flyers who can't afford to show up tired to board meetings, sales pitches, or conferences.
Yes — LayOvr has a free tier that includes the core circadian engine, single round-trip planning, manual flight entry, and all light/sleep/caffeine recommendations. The free tier is designed to feel complete, not like a teaser. Premium adds live flight lookup, real-time delay re-planning, multi-leg trips, HealthKit personalization, and unlimited saved trips.
LayOvr requires iOS 17 or later, running on any compatible iPhone. It is not currently available on iPad or Mac. An Apple Watch companion is planned for a future version.

The science

LayOvr's circadian engine implements the two-process model of sleep regulation (Process S — sleep pressure — and Process C — the circadian rhythm), combined with a Phase Response Curve (PRC) model for light and melatonin. The direction (east vs. west) and magnitude of your time zone shift are used to determine whether to advance or delay your clock, and by how much. The caffeine model accounts for adenosine receptor dynamics. The engine runs entirely offline in Swift — no server is required.
LayOvr is a wellness tool, not a medical device. It provides informational guidance based on published chronobiology. The recommendations are population-level baselines — your individual response may vary. Always consult your doctor if you have a sleep disorder, take medication affecting alertness or sleep, are pregnant, or have other medical conditions. See our Terms of Use for the full disclaimer.
LayOvr may suggest optimal melatonin timing as part of your circadian plan. This is informational only — the app does not recommend dosage. Melatonin's legal status and over-the-counter availability varies by country (it requires a prescription in some EU countries). Consult a pharmacist or physician before using melatonin.

Privacy & data

Only if you grant permission. LayOvr requests read-only access to Apple HealthKit (sleep analysis and HRV) to personalize the circadian model. This data never leaves your device — it is processed on-device only. You can revoke this permission anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → LayOvr.
All your trips, plans, and settings are stored locally on your iPhone. Ceven Labs does not operate a backend server that stores user data. The only external network call is the flight lookup API (premium), which sends only a flight number — no personal information. Read our full Privacy Policy.
Uninstalling LayOvr removes all stored data from your device. You can also reset all app data from the app's Settings tab. Since we don't store your data on our servers, there is nothing to request deletion from us directly.
